## Right to Repair

The r1mx project is aligned with the Right to Repair movement. The RED ONE and RED ONE MX
are fully discontinued, no longer under warranty, and no longer serviceable by the manufacturer.
Owners of these cameras have a legitimate interest in understanding and repairing their own equipment.

Relevant frameworks and resources:

| Resource | Description |
|---|---|
| [Right to Repair (iFixit)](https://www.ifixit.com/Right-to-Repair) | Right to repair advocacy and resources |
| [Electronic Frontier Foundation - Right to Repair](https://www.eff.org/issues/right-to-repair) | Digital rights perspective on repair |
